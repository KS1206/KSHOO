University Entry Requirements

Guidelines for Admission & Academic Integrity

Admission Requirements

Diploma Programmes

  • Pass SPM with minimum 3 credits including English or Math

Bachelor’s Degree Programmes

  • STPM or equivalent with minimum CGPA 2.00
  • Diploma in related field

Postgraduate Programmes

  • Bachelor’s Degree with CGPA 2.75 or above
  • Research programmes require proposal and supervisor

University Regulations

Attend at least 80% of all classes
No cheating, plagiarism, or academic dishonesty
Respect university staff, property, and peers
Follow dress code and campus etiquette
Maintain minimum CGPA of 2.00
Abide by all student conduct policies
